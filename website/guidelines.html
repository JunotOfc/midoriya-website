Community Guidelines:

So, for all Midoriya users and all affiliates to get the most out of it, the team has established some guidelines, crucial to having the healthiest community possible, without those toxic people! Our team does not actively monitor servers on which the Bot is present in any way, and is not responsible under any circumstances for unreasonable activities by server members in case of breach of guidelines. This responsibility rests with each Staff of each server. Of course, we recommend and expect these servers and users to follow our guidelines.

Guidelines

  • Types of cursing, insults or any sexualization of Midoriya
  • Spread hate, fake news, lies, in public or private places, about Midoriya and his team, even though what you said has already been denied.
  • Attempts to attack, hate, or the like, to Midoria's team or to himself

  • Getting advantages by bypassing Bot restrictions (Bugs, Rules, Guidelines, Create multiple accounts to earn more rewards, etc...)
  • Use of services provided by Midoriya and his team, in order to break these guidelines.
  • Any kind of fabrication or propagation of lies intended to say that you, your bot, and your team have any relationship or affiliation with Midoriya's team or himself, is prohibited. By the way, all our partners, and affiliates are listed on our support server!

  • Create bots identical to Midoriya, namely, "About Me", Name, Avatar and similar functions (if all the same)
  • Plagiarism. Obviously this is prohibited. You can get some features, layout of it, or some texts, but copying everything identically can be tantamount to banning, or even more severe punishments.
